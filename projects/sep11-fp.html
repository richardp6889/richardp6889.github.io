Butter Run

As part of the end of year project I choose the game engine ImpactJs which is to make a parkour platformer. The idea behind it was to make a piece of butter the character therefore being slippery. And the game will get progressively harder and amps the chanllenge of each level.

Hardest part was definitely getting the weltmeister to work but thanks to the help of Mr.Mueller which gave me the line `php -S locaslhost:8000` it resolved the trouble of not being able to interact with the buttons. The process to actually making it invovled making and finding sprites that matched with the vision of what I want in my game and the file neccessary to the core functions of the game are ready to be dealt with in weltmeister. The actually application of the entities as they are called in the level editor is the top layer and can be put anywhere which is how the player, level switching, and player dying are accomplished. Other layers were added to design the level and add a physical floor through the built-in collision layer which makes a barrier to where entities cannot pass through. The other layers were used to make the scenery and blocks not part of the floor. When put together they create something that I am proud of and if I would add something else it would be something interesting during the first level.
